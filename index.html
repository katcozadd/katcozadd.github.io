A little bit about me

Life has lead me a lot of crazy directions and I am thankful for that. I wouldn't be a Web Developer or Photographer without the twists and turns. Here are a few fun and constant facts about me...

TEA ENTHUSIAST

Half-british and definitely tea obsessed. Nothing makes me happier than a hot cup of Earl Grey on a cold rainy day or an iced Green Jasmine tea in the summer. Oh, and like every tea inbetween.

EXPLORER

Native to Colorado and undeniably in love with this state. I run away to the Gunnison Valley as much as possible to skip amongst the wildflowers and spend time with my family.

CHAMPION GOAT SHOWMAN

In the summer of 2007, I was the Colorado State Fair Grand Champion of Goat Showmanship... Don't believe me? Send me a message, I'll send you proof.

MASTER MARGARITA MAKER

In my free time, you can catch me drinking a margarita in the sun... or in the shade. I owe all my margarita expertise to all the time I spent bartending and serving at Comida at The Source in Denver.
